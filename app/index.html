Become a tutor at MSP.

Pick the course and tutorial group(s) you would like to tutor. Groups are assigned on a first-come, first-served basis — once a group is taken it disappears for everyone, so what you see is always live.

See available groups

How it works

A short, structured sign-up — no spreadsheets, no back-and-forth emails. The whole process takes a couple of minutes.

1 · Browse

Open the registration page, choose period 1 or 2, and expand a course to see its tutorial groups with days and times.

2 · Select

Tick the group(s) you want. We instantly flag any time clashes between your choices so you don't double-book yourself.

3 · Confirm

Add your details and submit. The group is locked to you immediately. If two people pick the same group at once, only the first is confirmed.

Who can tutor?

Tutoring positions are open to eligible PhD candidates, MSP alumni and staff. If you have been invited by the MSP office or by Matt Baker / Ian Anthony, you are in the right place. If you are unsure whether you are eligible, email the office before registering.
