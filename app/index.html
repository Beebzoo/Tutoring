How it works

A short, structured sign-up — no spreadsheets, no back-and-forth emails. The whole process takes a couple of minutes.

Step 1

Browse the timetable

Open the registration page and read the week at a glance — every open tutorial group, by day and time slot, for the period.

Step 2

Select your groups

Pick the group(s) you want. Time clashes between your choices are flagged instantly so you don't double-book yourself.

Step 3

Confirm

Add your details and submit. The group locks to you immediately. If two people pick the same group at once, only the first is confirmed.

Who can tutor?

Tutoring positions are open to eligible PhD candidates, MSP alumni and staff. If you have been invited by the MSP office or by Matt Baker / Ian Anthony, you are in the right place. If you are unsure whether you are eligible, email the office before registering.
